About Us

GrowGuardian is a project created by Seddik Ayoub and Ilyass Gouriny for the IoT: Design and Prototyping of Connected Devices course. Using key technologies like MQTT & IFTT, FlaskJS, and a RaspberryPi, we created an automated plant watering system.
